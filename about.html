Our Journey

2021: The Beginning

Founded in Kigali by a team of Rwandan entrepreneurs who saw the need for a local booking platform that truly understands Rwandan tourism.

2022: First 100 Properties

Onboarded our first 100 properties across Rwanda, focusing on Kigali and Musanze regions. Launched our mobile money payment system.

2023: Expansion

Expanded to cover all 5 provinces of Rwanda. Partnered with Rwanda Development Board to promote domestic tourism.

2024: Looking Ahead

Launching our mobile app, introducing experience bookings (gorilla trekking, cultural tours), and expanding to East African Community markets.
