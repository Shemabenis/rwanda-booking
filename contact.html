Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking by logging into your account, viewing your trips, and selecting "Cancel Booking". Cancellation fees may apply depending on the property policy.

When do I pay?

Payment policies vary by property. Some require prepayment, while others allow you to pay upon arrival.

Can I change my dates?

Yes, date changes are possible subject to availability and the property's policy.
